Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for one thing — playing your local music without getting in the way.

♪

Local Playback

Plays MP3, M4A, AAC, WAV, FLAC, AIFF and more. Your music, your files, no streaming required.

↻

Loop Modes

Loop a single track on repeat, loop your entire library, or play straight through. One click to cycle.

🔍

Instant Search

Filter your library by title, artist, or album. Results update as you type — find any track in seconds.

💾

Persistent Library

Your track list is saved automatically and survives app restarts. Deleted files are auto-pruned on launch.

🎨

Album Artwork

Extracts and displays embedded artwork as lazy-loaded thumbnails. Metadata for title, artist, album, and duration.

⏱

Lightweight

Under 8 MB memory for artwork cache. No Electron, no web views — pure native Swift and AppKit.

▶

Full Transport

Play, pause, next, previous, and smooth scrub seeking. Volume slider appears on hover — clean and minimal.

🎬

Dynamic Icon

Menubar shows a music note when idle and an animated waveform when playing — always know at a glance.

💻

Universal Binary

Runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs. Single binary, no Rosetta needed.
